# PO_CheckAdjacency

Analysis geometries (Curve, Brep, Extrusion, or Pollination object) for adjacencies.

Notes:
- The results are points that grouped by a test distance.

Tips:
- Use it to detect and fix apparent adjacencies of rooms.
